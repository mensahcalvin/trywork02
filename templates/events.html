Homowo Festival

Date: August 15-20, 2025

Location: Accra

Description: A harvest festival celebrated by the Ga people of Accra, featuring traditional dances, music, and the sprinkling of "kpokpoi" (a special dish).

Highlights:

  • Traditional drumming and dancing
  • Cultural displays
  • Food fairs
  • Street processions

The Aboakyer festival, celebrated on the first Saturday of May, is a vibrant event in Winneba, Ghana, marking the migration of the Efutu people from their ancestral homeland
