Trotro (Minibus) Information
Overview

Trotros are the most common form of public transportation in Ghana. They are minibuses that operate on fixed routes and are known for their affordability and availability.

Key Features
  • Most affordable transport option
  • Extensive network covering most areas
  • Frequent departures (every 5-15 minutes)
  • Shared rides with other passengers
Tips
  • Have small change ready for payment
  • Ask locals for help with routes
  • Be prepared for crowded conditions
Taxi Information
Overview

Taxis provide convenient and comfortable point-to-point transportation throughout Ghana.

Key Features
  • Available 24/7
  • Direct routes to your destination
  • Can be hailed or booked via app
  • More comfortable than public transport
Tips
  • Negotiate fare before starting journey
  • Use reputable taxi services
  • Share taxi for better rates
